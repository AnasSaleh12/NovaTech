Our story

Started in a spare bedroom, 2018

NovaTech opened with one laptop, one client, and a conviction that small studios do better work than big agencies pretending to be small. Since then we've stayed intentionally compact: senior designers and strategists only, no junior hand-offs, no account managers between you and the work.

We've since worked with founders across skincare, hospitality, fashion, and tech — but the way we work hasn't changed: small team, direct access, fast decisions.

Build brands good enough that competitors ask who did the work.

FAQ

Questions we get a lot

How long does a typical project take?

Brand identity projects usually run 6–8 weeks. Websites run 4–10 weeks depending on scope. We'll give you a firm timeline before any work starts.

How much does it cost?

Pricing depends on scope — a logo refresh costs far less than a full identity-plus-website build. We send a fixed quote after a short discovery call, so there are no surprises.

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Yes. About half our roster is early-stage founders. We offer scoped packages designed for teams that need to move fast on a tighter budget.

How many rounds of revisions are included?

Every project includes two structured revision rounds at each stage. Additional rounds are available and billed separately if needed.

Who owns the final files?

You do. Once the project is paid in full, all final source files and usage rights transfer to you outright.
